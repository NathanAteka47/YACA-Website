About Young Aviators Club of Africa

Welcome to the Young Aviators Club of Africa (YACA). We are a pan-African organization committed to nurturing the next generation of aviation professionals. Through our community-driven programs, mentorship, and events, we aim to promote interest in aviation among youth across the continent.

What We Do

Aviation Mentorship

Connecting youth with professionals across the aviation industry to inspire and guide their journey.

Flight Experiences

Offering first-hand flight experiences to build enthusiasm and understanding of aircraft operations.

Workshops & Seminars

Educational sessions covering aerodynamics, engineering, pilot training, and industry opportunities.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower the youth of Africa to pursue careers in aviation and aerospace through mentorship, education, and exposure.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading youth aviation platform, connecting young minds with opportunities in the global aviation industry.

Young Aviators Club of Africa is committed to raising future pilots, engineers, and aviation professionals from every corner of Africa.
