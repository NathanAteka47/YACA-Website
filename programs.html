Our Programs

At YACA, we are passionate about empowering African youth through aviation. Our diverse programs are designed to educate, inspire, and provide hands-on experience for aspiring aviators.

Aviation Mentorship

One-on-one guidance by experienced pilots, engineers, and aviation professionals to help students explore career paths and gain valuable insights.

School Outreach

Interactive workshops and presentations delivered in schools across Africa to ignite passion for aerospace careers among young learners.

Flight Experience

Hands-on training in flight simulators and real aircraft to provide practical exposure and build confidence for future aviators.

Aviation Bootcamps

Intensive, short-term programs focused on foundational skills, safety training, and aviation technology tailored for beginners.

Technical Workshops

Detailed sessions on aircraft maintenance, avionics, and engineering principles led by certified instructors and industry experts.
